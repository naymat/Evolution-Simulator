Introduction

This project attempts to explore and provide a simple implementation of the main principles of genetic aglgorithms. These principles being population, selection and mutation.

Sample Simulation:

Organisms, indicated by the red circles, compete with other organisms for food, which are represented by the small green circles. The more food an organism eats, the more fit it is. The most fit organisms have the highest chance of reproducing.

Although organisms which eat more food have a higher chance to appear in the next generation, this is not guarenteed. This program uses a Roulette Wheel Selection aglgorithm to determine which organism is selected
